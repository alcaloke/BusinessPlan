Introduction

The aim is to build a working prototype of a Phoneticon Interface for the chrome browser. The prototype interface will feature sufficient functionalities to give the user a taste of creating audiovisual texts, or, phoneticons. The main purpose of the interface is to provide a new medium for digital communication, where the expression of the text is expanded to the realms of sound & music, form & colour, as well as, motion & physics.
